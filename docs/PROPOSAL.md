## Concept

Mind Mirror is a learning platform that reflects a learner's gaps in understanding.
It serves two distinct user groups: **Students** and **Teachers/Lecturers**.

## Student side

1. **Create a learning context**
   - Upload learning materials (PDF, JPEG) so the AI understands the scope of the content accurately.

2. **Explain content in their own words**
   - Input via: writing (note photo upload), typing, or speaking (audio).

3. **Identify blind spots and misconceptions**
   - Output as a **Knowledge Map**: subtopics connected to reveal relationships across the
     whole lesson as a complete knowledge structure.

4. **Personalized learning assessment**
   - Generate questions tailored to what the learner truly does not understand / is confused about.
   - Answers via typing, speaking, or writing. **No multiple choice.**
   - Goal: assess conceptual understanding.

## Teacher side

1. **Teacher Dashboard**
   - Reports each student's understanding state.
   - Shows topics understood / confused / not understood **before** assessment.
   - Shows post-assessment scores and whether previously not-understood topics improved,
     and which topics are still not understood.
   - Helps teachers adjust teaching per student.

2. **Long-term understanding pie chart**
   - Per studied topic, e.g. Functions: understood 70%, confused 20%, not understood 10%.
